Biography of an Orange Popsicle

Honeybee joined the Rasmussen cat horde October 2022. She came from a rescue where they told her story. She was a young kitten when she went into her first heat. In this first heat, she immediately got pregnant and gave birth. Her litter passed away. She recovered and went into her next heat a mere month after her first birth. Again, she got pregnant and again her litter all passed away during birth. This extra young mother was grieving and depressed. She went into the rescue and thankfully was fixed. Even so, she mourned the loss of her littles and it was apparent in her demeanor. Courtney went to the rescue with her husband, suffering grief of her own. Honeybee was taken to her forever home with the Rasmussens and there she resides with her best friend, Aussie, who helped her endure and recover. Honeybee was scared at first. Even now, she is standoffish to people but she has found solace in Courtney and Aussie. Now Honeybee is excited about life and thrives on treats. She loves to watch TV, especially Peppa Pig.
